description=[h2]📦 What is this mod?[/h2]
description=The world didn't end with a bang. It ended with a whimper — and then it started to rot. [b]World Decay[/b] transforms Knox County into a crumbling, overgrown wasteland where every corner shows the scars of abandonment. Cracked roads, collapsed roofs, shattered windows, rusted vehicles, and vegetation that devours everything in its path.
description=
description=This mod transforms Project Zomboid into a true post-apocalyptic wasteland where nature and entropy are the dominant forces. Every corner of the map shows the scars of abandonment: overgrown roads, collapsed roofs, burned walls, shattered windows, rusted vehicles, and the quiet remnants of those who didn't make it.
description=
description=[h2]🌟 Key Features[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*][b]Advanced Vegetation System:[/b] Trees, bushes, grass, and vines grow everywhere with fully configurable percentages through sandbox options.
description=[*][b]Urban Decay:[/b] Cracked roads, damaged walls, destroyed doors and windows, roof collapses, and burned structures.
description=[*][b]Abandoned Vehicles:[/b] Wrecked cars scattered across the map with configurable rarity.
description=[*][b]Environmental Storytelling:[/b] Random narrative scenes that tell the stories of survivors who didn't make it.
description=[*][b]Barricades & Defenses:[/b] Remnants of desperate last stands found throughout the world.
description=[*][b]Trash & Debris:[/b] Accumulated garbage that reinforces the passage of time.
description=[*][b]Cleaning System:[/b] Right-click menu to remove vegetation and clean up areas for rebuilding.
description=[*][b]Full Sandbox Customization:[/b] Every element can be tuned, enabled, or disabled to your preference.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]⚡ Performance Notice[/h2]
description=[b]⚠️ IMPORTANT:[/b] This mod significantly alters the world generation and adds a large amount of objects to every loaded chunk. As a result, it may [b]negatively impact performance[/b] on lower-end systems. This is completely normal due to how Project Zomboid handles object rendering and is not a bug.
description=
description=[b]🕐 First Load:[/b] It is completely normal to experience some lag during the first few seconds after loading into the world, as vegetation and objects are being generated across all visible chunks. Give it a moment — performance will stabilize once the initial generation pass completes.
description=
description=Recommendations:
description=[list]
description=[*]Allocate more RAM to the game if possible.
description=[*]Reduce vegetation percentages in sandbox options if you experience lag.
description=[*]In multiplayer, the host/server specs matter greatly.
description=[/list]
