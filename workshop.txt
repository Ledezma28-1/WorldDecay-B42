description=[h2]📦 What is this mod?[/h2]
description=[b]World Decay[/b] overrides the base map appearance to reflect [b]years of urban decay[/b]. When each map chunk loads, the mod applies its generators [b]procedurally[/b]: vegetation (trees, bushes, grass, vines), structural decay (cracks, burned walls, broken doors and windows, bent fences), trash, and barricades.
description=
description=Uses a [b]persistent chunk cache[/b] to skip already-visited areas and a [b]batched dispatcher[/b] to avoid lag spikes. Includes cleaning tools and [b]full sandbox control[/b] over every element.
description=
description=[h2]🌟 Key Features[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*][b]Vegetation System:[/b] Trees, bushes, grass, and vines with configurable percentages via sandbox.
description=[*][b]Urban Decay:[/b] Cracked roads, burned walls, broken doors and windows, bent and broken fences.
description=[*][b]Barricades:[/b] Remnants of desperate last stands found throughout the world.
description=[*][b]Trash & Debris:[/b] Accumulated garbage that reinforces the passage of time.
description=[*][b]Cleaning System:[/b] Right-click menu to remove vegetation and clear areas for rebuilding.
description=[*][b]Full Sandbox Customization:[/b] Every element can be tuned, enabled, or disabled.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]⚡ Performance Notice[/h2]
description=[b]⚠️ IMPORTANT:[/b] This mod significantly alters the world generation and adds a large amount of objects to every loaded chunk. As a result, it may [b]negatively impact performance[/b] on lower-end systems. This is completely normal due to how Project Zomboid handles object rendering and is not a bug.
description=
description=[b]🕐 First Load:[/b] It is completely normal to experience some lag during the first few seconds after loading into the world, as vegetation and objects are being generated across all visible chunks. Give it a moment — performance will stabilize once the initial generation pass completes.
description=
description=Recommendations:
description=[list]
description=[*]Allocate more RAM to the game if possible.
description=[*]Reduce vegetation percentages in sandbox options if you experience lag.
description=[*]In multiplayer, the host/server specs matter greatly.
description=[/list]
